Details

With 20 years' experience in providing a great service, Maple Manor valet parking is a firm favourite with our customers and it's easy to see why. One of their professional, insured chauffeurs will meet you at the terminal and park your car for you in a secured car park while you catch your flight, then have it waiting for you when you get back. It couldn't be simpler and all for this low price. We only sell airport-approved Meet and Greet services and Maple Manor is no exception.

Your car will be washed, rinsed and dried by hand, the inside will be vacuumed, the wheels will be washed and the dashboard, vinyl and leather will be wiped clean. The valet does not include the inside of the boot, however.
When you book, you'll be asked what time you'd like your chauffeur to meet you. If you'd like to change the time, please call Maple Manor on 01293 652299, and choose option 3, at least 48 hours before you're due to travel.
If you need any extra days' parking, the car park will charge you ÃÂ£7.50 per day or part day. If you don't use all your parking, unfortunately we can't give you a refund for the unused days.
Please make sure you leave enough time for your chauffeur to check your car for damage. It will only take a few minutes, but if you don't leave enough time, this may affect any claim you later make for damage.
It is your responsibility to make sure your car is roadworthy and has a valid MOT certificate and tax disc. If your car isn't legal or safe to drive, Maple Manor won't be able to drive it to the car park, and you also won't be able to get a refund.
In the unlikely event that the monorail should break down, there will be a bus between the two terminals, which will take slightly longer. However, as long as you arrive at the airport at least two hours before your flight, as is recommended anyway, you should have plenty of time.[/date]

This car park is appropriate for those staying at the South terminal.

Arrival

You should call Maple Manor on 01293 652299 and choose option 1 when you're about 10 to 15 minutes from the terminal (which will normally be when you're on the M23). If you're running more than 15 minutes early or late, you should let Maple Manor know.

Your chauffeur will meet you at the terminal's Approved Operator drop-off area. He or she will be wearing a uniform, and will help you unload your luggage and check your car for damage. You'll then need to give your chauffeur your car keys - you'll want to separate them from the rest of your keys - and they will take your car to Maple Manor's secured car park while you head to check-in. Please have your return flight details and booking confirmation to hand when you meet the driver.

Departures

Once you've collected your luggage, call 01293 652299 and choose option 1. Once you've made your way through the security checks, go to the Approved Operator meeting point outside arrivals. Your car will be brought to the same place where you dropped it off, unless you're at a different terminal.

Directions

As you arrive at the terminal, keep in the right-hand lane. Follow either of the passenger drop off lanes round to the right, continue to the far end of the tunnel and pull over on the left-hand side of the road.
