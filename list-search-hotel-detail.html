Renaissance Riverside Saigon Overview

The hotel comprises a total of 349 rooms and guests are offered use of a spacious foyer with a 24-hour reception desk, a currency exchange desk and a conference room. There is also a cosy bar and a restaurant that affords views out over the pleasant swimming pool. Car parking facilities are to be found outside. This exclusive luxury hotel is located at the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, just a few minutes from the town centre where guests will discover a wide selection of shopping and entertainment venues. Wonderful panoramic views are to be had over the Saigon River from the hotel and Tan Son Nhat airport is to be found about 20 minutes drive away. The comfortable, modern rooms come with their own bathroom with hairdryer, a direct dial telephone, satellite/cable TV, a minibar and air conditioning. There is a swimming pool in the outdoor complex and guests may make use of the extensive spa treatments such as a massage service and steam baths.

Don't be put off by the reviews

James, Kent, England

8/10 Great

20 May 2011

Young Couple (18-35)

We've just returned from a 5 night stay at the Pennsylvania. We had to book quickly after a different accommodation booking fell through. After we'd booked, we read the pages and pages of dire reviews on the Pennsylvania and dreaded what lay ahead. We arrived at the hotel. The wait at reception was brief and the staff were friendly and polite. We had 2 rooms booked and made our way to the first, worried of what would we'd find when we opened the door! The room was perfectly acceptable - I can't deny that it's in need of bringing up to date. However it was clean and comfortable. The same was true of both rooms. The housekeeping staff we friendly, the towels were replaced daily and the rooms left clean and tidied. I don't normally write reviews - but after returning from a very pleasant stay - I felt I had to offer my view. As I say - it's a little tired around the edges. But the main priorities - clean, safe and comfortable are all checked. Make the most of the amazing, central location. Have a brilliant holiday and don't worry - the hotel really isn't that bad.
